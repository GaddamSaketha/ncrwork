x[8]= {10,20,30,40,50,60,70,80};
Analyze the meaning of below statements
a. What is the meaning of x?
b. What is the meaning of (x+2)?
c. What is the value of *x?
d. What is the value of (*x+2)?
e. What is the value of *(x+2)?

Solution:
a)x denotes the name of the array which stores the base address of the array
b)x+2 gives the address of the element 30 in the array
c)10
d)12
e)30
